About Us

Our Story, Our Impact

LeafCalc was born from a shared vision between four IIT bombay students who saw a critical gap between corporate climate ambition and accessible, data-driven action. Our mission is to empower organizations of every size to seamlessly measure their complete carbon footprint and translate complex data into a clear, actionable roadmap for a sustainable future. We build tools that don't just report on the past; they help you build a greener tomorrow.
